Dog Licensing

License your dog and learn about pet regulations in Wellsville

The City of Wellsville appreciates responsible pet owners! We depend on pet owners to keep their dogs properly vaccinated and licensed.

Licensing for cats is currently not necessary.

Dog licensing is required by law

Citations may be issued for non-compliance.

Registration Requirements

All dogs weaned or 6 months of age or older must be registered.

Licensing for the year occurs from January through March.

After March 31st, dog licensing fees triple, unless it is a newly acquired dog or you are new to the area.

Required Documents

To receive a dog license, please provide the following:

  1. A copy of a current rabies vaccination certificate from your veterinarian, showing that your dog is current on its vaccine.
  2. Proof that the animal has been spayed or neutered, to receive the discounted fee.
  3. The appropriate fee:
    • ${{ spayedNeuteredFee }}/year for spayed/neutered animals
    • ${{ unalteredFee }}/year for unaltered animals
    • This fee is per animal.

License Tag

The dog owner is then given an identification tag, which is maintained in an animal identification report to assist the animal control officer in quickly returning lost pets to their owners.

Important: The rabies tag that you received from your vet is not a license.

City ordinance requires that all dogs be licensed. This also allows your pet to be returned safely to you in the event that it is lost.
