Important Licensing Requirements

License Required by Law

All businesses operating in Wellsville require a business license. Failure to obtain a license is a Class B misdemeanor.

Annual Renewal

Business licenses run from January 1 through December 31 each year. Fees are due before each calendar year and become delinquent if not paid by February 1st.

City Council Approval

All new business license applications require approval from the Wellsville City Council. You will be scheduled to appear before the council for review.

Home-Based Businesses

Home occupancy businesses require additional approval from the Planning and Zoning Commission through a public hearing process.
