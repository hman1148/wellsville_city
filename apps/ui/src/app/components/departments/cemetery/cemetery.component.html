Cemetery

Welcome to Wellsville Cemetery

The Wellsville Cemetery contains approximately ten acres and has about 7,700 plots. It is owned and maintained by Wellsville City. The grounds are located on Center Street between 200 South and 300 South.

Cemetery Hours

The cemetery is open from daybreak to dusk.

Decoration Policy

Decorations are allowed to remain on graves through the following periods, after which they will be removed by cemetery staff:

  • Easter: Two weeks after Easter Sunday
  • Memorial Day: Two weeks after Memorial Day
  • Independence Day: Two weeks after July 4th
  • Christmas: First Thursday after New Year's Day

Note: The cemetery is mowed and trimmed every Thursday. All decorations will be removed from graves on Thursday to facilitate proper maintenance.

Additional Policies
  • Vehicles: Motor vehicles are not allowed in the cemetery except for burial services and official business.
  • Animals: Animals, except service animals, are not permitted in the cemetery.
  • Children: Children must be supervised by an adult at all times.
  • Burial Restrictions: Burials are not conducted on Sundays or legal holidays.

Regulations for Markers and Monuments

Upright Monuments

Upright monuments must be set on concrete foundations. The foundation must extend at least 6 inches below ground level and be at least 4 inches thick. The monument must be set back at least 12 inches from the front of the plot.

Ground-Level Flush Markers

Ground-level flush markers must be made of granite, bronze, or other approved materials. They must be set flush with the ground surface to allow for proper maintenance and mowing.

Concrete Borders

Concrete borders around plots are permitted but must be installed flush with the ground. Raised borders are not allowed as they interfere with maintenance equipment.

Material Recommendations

Due to weather conditions and longevity, granite is the recommended material for monuments and markers. Marble and softer stones may deteriorate over time and are not recommended for outdoor use.

For specific questions about monument installation or to schedule monument placement, please contact the Wellsville City office.
